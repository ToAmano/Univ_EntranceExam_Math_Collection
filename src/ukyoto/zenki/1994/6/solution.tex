[解] $P(\theta) = (x(\theta), y(\theta))$ とおく。又、以下 $\sin\theta = S, \cos\theta = C$ とする
(1) $x'(\theta) = -2S + 2\sin 2\theta, \ y'(\theta) = 2C - 2\cos 2\theta$ より
\begin{align*}
L &= \int_0^{2\pi} \sqrt{\{x'(\theta)\}^2 + \{y'(\theta)\}^2} d\theta \\
&= 2 \int_0^{2\pi} \sqrt{2 - 2\cos\theta} \ d\theta \\
&= 2 \int_0^{2\pi} 2 \left| \sin\frac{\theta}{2} \right| d\theta \\
&= 4 \int_0^\pi |\sin t| \cdot 2\ dt \quad \left(t = \frac{\theta}{2}\right) \\
&= 8 \big[ -\cos t \big]_0^\pi = 16
\end{align*}

(2) (1)から
$$8 \int_0^{\frac{1}{2}\theta_n} |\sin t| dt = \frac{16}{n}$$
$0 < \frac{1}{2}\theta_n < \pi$ から
$$8 \left( 1 - \cos\frac{\theta_n}{2} \right) = \frac{16}{n}$$
$$\therefore n = \frac{2}{1 - \cos\frac{\theta_n}{2}} = \frac{1}{\sin^2\frac{\theta_n}{4}}$$
だから $\sin\frac{\theta_n}{4} > 0$ から
$$\sqrt{n}\ \theta_n = \frac{\theta_n}{\sin\frac{\theta_n}{4}} = \frac{\frac{1}{4}\theta_n}{\sin\frac{1}{4}\theta_n} \cdot 4 \to 4$$
$(n \to \infty \text{ で } \theta_n \to 0)$
