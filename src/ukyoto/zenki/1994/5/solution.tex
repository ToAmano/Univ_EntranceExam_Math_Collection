上図から
\begin{align*}
a_{n+1} &= \frac{1}{3} b_n + \frac{2}{3} c_n \\
b_{n+1} &= \frac{1}{3} a_n + \frac{2}{3} b_n \\
c_{n+1} &= \frac{2}{3} a_n + \frac{1}{3} c_n
\end{align*}

(2) $a_n + b_n + c_n = 1$ から $c_n$ を消去し(1)に代入して
\begin{equation}
a_{n+1} = \frac{1}{3}(-2a_n - b_n + 2) \tag{①}
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
b_{n+1} = \frac{1}{3}a_n + \frac{2}{3}b_n \tag{②}
\end{equation}
①から $b_n = -3a_{n+1} - 2a_n + 2$ を②に代入
$$-3a_{n+2} - 2a_{n+1} + 2 = \frac{1}{3}a_n + \frac{2}{3}(-3a_{n+1} - 2a_n + 2)$$
$$9a_{n+2} = 3a_n + 2$$
$$a_{n+2} - \frac{1}{3} = \frac{1}{3} \left(a_n - \frac{1}{3}\right)$$

これと $a_0 = 1, a_1 = 0$ から、等比数列の公式から
$$a_{2k} = \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k \frac{2}{3} + \frac{1}{3}$$
$$a_{2k+1} = \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^k \left(-\frac{1}{3}\right) + \frac{1}{3}$$
だから
$$a_n = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{3}\left[2\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{\frac{n}{2}} + 1\right] & (n \in \text{even}) \\ \frac{1}{3}\left[-\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{\frac{n-1}{2}} + 1\right] & (n \in \text{odd}) \end{cases}$$
