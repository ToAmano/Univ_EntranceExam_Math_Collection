[\textbf{解}] $A\left(-\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{4}\right)$, $B\left(\frac{3}{2}, \frac{9}{4}\right)$, $P(x, x^2)$, $f(x) = \overline{PA}^2 + \overline{PB}^2$ とおく.

\[
f(x) = \left(x + \frac{1}{2}\right)^2 + \left(x^2 - \frac{1}{4}\right)^2 + \left(x - \frac{3}{2}\right)^2 + \left(x^2 - \frac{9}{4}\right)^2
\]
\[
= 2x^4 - 3x^2 - 2x + \frac{61}{8}
\]

\[
f'(x) = 8x^3 - 6x - 2
\]
\[
f''(x) = 24x^2 - 6 = 6(2x+1)(2x-1)
\]

以下表を得る:

\[
\begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c}
x & \dots & -\frac{1}{2} & \dots & \frac{1}{2} & \dots & 1 & \dots \\ \hline
f' & - & - & - & - & - & 0 & + \\ \hline
f'' & + & 0 & - & 0 & + & + & + \\ \hline
f & \searrow & & \searrow & & \searrow & \text{極小} & \nearrow
\end{array}
\]

よって, グラフは下図:
