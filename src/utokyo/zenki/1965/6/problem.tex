右の図は半径の長さ1の半円で，弦 $AP$，$AQ$ と直径 $AB$ とのつくる角はそれぞれ $30^\circ$，$60^\circ$ である．

このとき，弦 $AP$，$AQ$ と円弧 $PQ$ とで囲まれる部分を直径 $AB$ のまわりに一回転して得られる立体の体積を求めよ．

\begin{figure}[htbp]
  \centering
  \begin{tikzpicture}[scale=1.5, >=stealth]
    \coordinate (A) at (-1.5,0);
    \coordinate (B) at (1.5,0);
    \coordinate (O) at (0,0);

    \draw[thick] (A) -- (B);
    \draw[thick] (1.5,0) arc (0:180:1.5);

    \coordinate (P) at ($(A) + (30:{3.0*cos(30)})$);
    \coordinate (Q) at ($(A) + (60:{3.0*cos(60)})$);

    \draw[thick] (A) -- (P);
    \draw[thick] (A) -- (Q);

    \fill (P) circle (1.2pt) node[above right] {$P$};
    \fill (Q) circle (1.2pt) node[above left] {$Q$};

    \node[below left] at (A) {$A$};
    \node[below right] at (B) {$B$};
  \end{tikzpicture}
\end{figure}
