下の図は線分 $AB$ の投影図で，その各部の寸法は図に記入してある通りである．

この線分の上端 $A$ を通り，$AB$ となす角が $60^\circ$，
平画面に対する傾きが $30^\circ$ であるような直線の，平画面上の跡を $C$ とする．
点 $B$ の平面図 $b$ を原点とし，$ab$ を $x$ 軸，$bb'$ を $y$ 軸として，点 $C$ の座標を求めよ．

\begin{figure}[htbp]
  \centering
  \begin{tikzpicture}[scale=1.5, >=stealth]
    \draw[line width=0.5pt] (-1.5,0) node[left] {$X$} -- (2.5,0) node[right] {$Y$};

    \coordinate (Bp) at (0,0);
    \coordinate (Ap) at (1.5, 1.5);
    \draw[thick] (Bp) -- (Ap) -- (1.5, 0);

    \node[left] at (Bp) {$b'$};
    \node[above] at (Ap) {$a'$};

    \draw[dashed, decorate, decoration={brace, raise=4pt}] (1.5,0) -- (Ap) node[midway, right=6pt] {$1$};

    \coordinate (B) at (0, -1.5);
    \coordinate (A) at (1.5, -1.5);
    \draw[thick] (0,0) -- (B) -- (A) -- (1.5,0);

    \node[below left] at (B) {$b$};
    \node[below right] at (A) {$A$};

    \draw[dashed, decorate, decoration={brace, mirror, raise=4pt}] (0,0) -- (B) node[midway, left=6pt] {$1$};
    \draw[dashed, decorate, decoration={brace, mirror, raise=4pt}] (1.5,0) -- (A) node[midway, right=6pt] {$1$};
    \draw[dashed, decorate, decoration={brace, mirror, raise=4pt}] (B) -- (A) node[midway, below=6pt] {$1$};
  \end{tikzpicture}
\end{figure}
