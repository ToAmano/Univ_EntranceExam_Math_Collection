(1) $S_1 = \alpha+\beta = 4, \quad S_2 = \alpha^2+\beta^2 = (\alpha+\beta)^2-2\alpha\beta = 18, \quad S_3 = (\alpha+\beta)^3-3\alpha\beta(\alpha+\beta) = 76$

$$S_n = (\alpha+\beta)(\alpha^{n-1}+\beta^{n-1}) - \alpha\beta(\alpha^{n-2}+\beta^{n-2}) = 4S_{n-1} + S_{n-2}$$
