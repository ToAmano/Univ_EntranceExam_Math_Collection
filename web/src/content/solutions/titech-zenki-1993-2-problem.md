$n$を自然数とする．

1.  $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin (2n+1)x}{\sin x}$を求めよ．

2.  $\displaystyle \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \frac{\sin (2n+1)x}{\sin x}dx = \frac{\pi}{2}$を示せ．
